## **Additional Features for an Academic Collaborative Framework**

### **1. Content Management & Enhancement**

* **Multiple file formats** support (PDF, PPTX, DOCX, MP4, links to external content).
* **Version control** for uploaded materials.
* **Automatic transcription** and **caption generation** for videos.
* **Document previews** (slides preview, PDF viewer, video thumbnails).
* **Tagging system** for courses/topics/concepts.
* **Metadata extraction** from uploaded files (title, keywords, slide headings).

---

### **2. Course & Material Organization**

* Ability for professors to **create course pages** that group multiple files.
* **Course descriptions**, objectives, prerequisites.
* **Syllabus upload** or inline editor.
* **Semester-based organization** (Fall 2024, Spring 2025, etc.).

---

### **3. Collaboration & Interaction**

* **Comments** and threaded discussions under materials.
* **Q&A section** for each course/material.
* **Feedback or rating system** beyond likes (e.g., “clarity”, “difficulty”, “usefulness”).
* **Peer reviews** for professors to review and endorse each other’s materials.
* **Messaging** or communication channels (with privacy controls).

---

### **4. User Roles & Access Control**

* Add roles such as:

  * **System Admin**
  * **Professor**
  * **Teaching Assistant**
  * **Student**
* Resource-based permission controls:

  * Who can upload?
  * Who can comment?
  * Who can edit/delete content?

---

### **5. Search & Discovery Enhancements**

* **Advanced search** with filters:

  * Course level (undergrad/grad)
  * Discipline
  * Keywords/tags
  * Professor name
* **Natural language search** (semantic search using embeddings).
* **Recommended materials** based on:

  * Student profile
  * Course history
  * Popularity trends.

---

### **6. Analytics & Insights**

* View counters for each material.
* Download statistics.
* Student engagement analytics:

  * Time spent on slides/videos.
  * Trending courses.
* Professor dashboard showing:

  * Student interactions
  * Feedback summaries
  * Most viewed materials.

---

### **7. Academic Integrity & Verification**

* **Verification of faculty** (automated email verification or admin approval).
* **Flagging**

  * Plagiarism flags
  * Inappropriate or outdated content
* **Citation tools** for students to reference materials.

---

### **8. Integrations & Interoperability**

* Integrations with LMS systems (Canvas, Moodle, Blackboard).
* Integration with cloud storage (Google Drive, OneDrive).
* Embeddable widgets for external sites.
* Single Sign-On (SSO) using university credentials.

---

### **9. Mobile & Accessibility**

* Responsive design or dedicated mobile app.
* Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA).
* Support for screen readers, captions, transcripts.

---

### **10. Gamification & Community Features**

* Badges for:

  * Active professors
  * Helpful students
  * High-quality content
* Leaderboards (optional, with privacy controls)
* Achievement milestones for contributors.

---

### **11. Monetization Options (Optional)**

* Donation-based materials
* Sponsored university pages
* Premium features for institutions (analytics, large storage, SSO, etc.)

---

### **12. System Administration Tools**

* Admin dashboard for managing:

  * Users
  * Lookup data (universities, countries, departments)
  * Content moderation
  * Reports and analytics
* Backup and archival tools.
* Storage quota management.
